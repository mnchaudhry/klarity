## Database Schema (Supabase)

**users** table
- id, email, created_at
- streak_start_date (timestamp)
- why_im_doing_this (text)
- current_streak_days (int)
- longest_streak_days (int)

**struggle_sessions** table
- id, user_id, created_at
- trigger_type (enum: bored | stressed | lonely | spontaneous)
- outcome (enum: overcame | relapsed)
- duration_seconds (int)
- ai_messages (jsonb — full conversation stored)

**checkins** table
- id, user_id, date
- mood (int 1–5)
- note (text, optional)

**journal_entries** table
- id, user_id, created_at
- content (text)

### `/struggling` — The Spike Intervention Flow

**This is the most critical UX in the entire app. Every millisecond matters.**

The flow is a series of full-screen "steps" — no scrolling, one thing at a time, transitions handled by Framer Motion.

---

**Step 0 — Pattern Interrupt Screen** *(appears instantly, 0 delay)*

Full screen takeover. Background shifts to deep navy or dark teal — different from the rest of the app on purpose.

Center of screen:
- Their streak in large text: *"47 days."*
- Below it: *"That's real. That's yours."*
- Below that, smaller: *[their "why" text from onboarding — shown in quotes]*
- Wait 2–3 seconds (auto-advance or tap to continue)

This forced pause is intentional. It's the most important moment.

---

**Step 1 — Trigger Check** *(5 seconds to complete)*

Headline: *"What's going on right now?"*

4 large tap buttons, full width, stacked:
- 😴 *Bored / nothing to do*
- 😰 *Stressed / overwhelmed*
- 😔 *Lonely / disconnected*
- ⚡ *It just hit me out of nowhere*

One tap → immediately moves to Step 2. No confirm button.

---

**Step 2 — AI Chat** *(core of the flow)*

**UI:**
- Chat interface, bottom-up (messages appear from bottom)
- AI message appears first — pre-written based on their trigger selection, arrives instantly (no loading wait for first message)
- Examples:
  - Bored → *"Okay. Boredom is one of the sneakiest triggers — the brain looks for stimulation. You're not weak, you're just understimulated right now. Tell me what's around you."*
  - Stressed → *"You're carrying something heavy right now. That makes sense. Before anything else — what's actually stressing you out? Say it out loud, even just to me."*
  - Lonely → *"Loneliness hits hard. It's one of the most human feelings there is. I'm here right now. What's making you feel disconnected today?"*
  - Spontaneous → *"It came out of nowhere — that happens. Your brain is looking for a shortcut to feel good. Let's slow this down together. What were you doing just before?"*

- User types reply → real Claude API call from /api/chat
- System prompt for Claude (hardcoded, not visible to user):
  - Role: compassionate recovery coach
  - Goal: keep user engaged and talking for 10–15 minutes
  - Never shame, never lecture
  - Suggest physical redirects naturally within conversation
  - After 3-4 exchanges, gently ask how they're feeling now vs when they opened the app
  - If they say they're feeling better, celebrate and guide to resolution

**Chat input:** Simple text box, fixed at bottom, send button. Auto-focus on load.

**Top of chat screen:**
- Small subtle bar showing their streak: *"47 days at stake"*
- X button top right — if they tap it, confirm dialog: *"Are you sure you want to leave? Your streak is still intact."*

---

**Step 3 — Physical Redirect Card** *(offered by AI naturally in conversation, or shown as suggestion card after 2 minutes)*

A card slides up from bottom mid-conversation:
- Headline: *"Want to break the physical state?"*
- 3 quick options:
  - 💧 *Splash cold water on your face*
  - 🏃 *Do 20 pushups or jumping jacks*
  - 🚶 *Walk to a different room right now*
- Tapping one acknowledges it, card dismisses, chat continues

---

**Step 4 — Resolution Screen**

After conversation, AI naturally leads to: *"How are you feeling compared to when you opened the app?"*

Then AI presents or user can manually tap a floating button:

Two options, full width:
- ✅ *"I got through it"* — green
- 💔 *"I relapsed"* — muted, not punishing red

**If "Got through it":**
- Full screen celebration — confetti (Framer Motion), large text: *"You did it."*
- Subline: *"Streak intact. That craving is gone forever."*
- Small text: *"Struggles overcome: 12"* (increments)
- Button: *"Back to home"*

**If "Relapsed":**
- No harsh colors. Muted screen.
- Text: *"It's okay. One moment doesn't define you."*
- Below: *"Your streak resets. But everything you learned doesn't."*
- AI sends one final message: warm, no shame, forward-looking
- Button: *"Start again"* → resets streak_start_date in Supabase, goes to dashboard

### `/patterns` Patterns Screen

**Purpose:** Help user understand themselves over time

**Charts (Recharts):**
1. **Trigger Breakdown** — donut chart. Which triggers come up most (Bored / Stressed / Lonely / Spontaneous)
2. **Time of Day** — bar chart. When do struggles tend to happen (morning / afternoon / evening / night)
3. **Day of Week** — bar chart. Which days are hardest
4. **Mood vs Relapse** — simple correlation. Low mood days = higher struggle frequency

- Example: *"Most of your struggles happen on Sunday evenings. That's worth knowing."*
- Example: *"Stress is your most common trigger — 60% of your sessions."*

## API Route — `/api/chat`

- Receives: messages array + trigger type + user's "why" text + streak days
- System prompt constructed server-side (never exposed to client)
- Calls Claude claude-sonnet-4-20250514
- Streams response back to client (useStream or fetch with ReadableStream)
- Saves completed conversation to struggle_sessions table in Supabase

## Key Design Decisions

- **Dark theme only.** This app gets opened at night, in bed, in vulnerable moments. Dark theme is respectful of that context.
- **No social features.** No leaderboards, no sharing, no community. Privacy is the product.
- **No gamification beyond the streak.** No badges, no points. They cheapen the gravity of what this is.
- **Framer Motion used specifically for:** streak number animation, step transitions in /struggling, celebration confetti, pattern interrupt screen transition
- **Mobile-first layout** even as a web app — max-width 430px centered, everything thumb-reachable
- **No ads, ever.** Monetize via a simple one-time purchase or small monthly subscription to unlock patterns + journal AI features. Free tier gets streak + AI chat.

## Color Palette

**Philosophy:** Dark, calm, and trustworthy. Feels like a quiet room at night — safe, grounding, serious but not cold.

---

**Primary Background** `#0A0A0F`
Near black with a very slight blue undertone.

**Secondary Background** `#12121A`
Cards, bottom nav, input areas.

**Surface / Elevated** `#1C1C28`
Modals, expanded cards, sheet overlays.

---

**Accent — Primary** `#2A9D8F`
Deep teal. The main interactive color — buttons, active nav icons, streak ring. Teal feels calm, focused, and forward-moving. Associated with clarity of mind and self-control.

**Accent — Streak Gold** `#F5A623`
Used only for streak number and ring at 30+ days. Earned, not given.

**Accent — Streak Blue** `#4A90D9`
Streak ring at 7–29 days. Steady, calm progress.

---

**The Struggling Button** `#C0392B` → gradient to `#8E1A10`
Deep red. Serious and urgent without being panicked. A lifeline, not an alarm.

**Pattern Interrupt Screen** `#0D1B2A`
Deep navy. Only used in /struggling flow. The color shift signals to the brain: *pay attention.*

---

**Success / Overcame** `#27AE60`
Clean green. "I got through it" button and celebration screen.

**Relapse / Reset** `#5D5D6B`
Muted gray. Not red — deliberately unsensational. No punishment, just reset.

---

**Text — Primary** `#F0F0F5`
Slightly warm white. Easier on the eyes at night.

**Text — Secondary** `#7A8A99`
Muted blue-gray for subtitles, metadata, timestamps.

**Text — Disabled / Placeholder** `#3A4450`
